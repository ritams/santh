# M. S. Santhanam

## About

Welcome to the webpage of Santhanam.

He is on the faculty of physics at Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Pune, India.

## Research Interests

Research interests broadly cover the areas of:
- Nonlinear dynamics
- Quantum chaos
- Quantum computation and machine learning
- Statistical physics
- Complex networks
- Extreme events
- Complex systems

Specifically, the problems range from driven quantum systems and random matrix theory to random walks and extreme events on networks and data science.

For more information, visit the research and publications pages.
